ABOUT ME

WHO AM I?

The man behind the code

A highly motivated 23-year-old from Puerto Rico with a passion for photography and a technical mind. Fueled by a desire to understand how things work, I'm actively learning web development (HTML, CSS) to translate creative vision into user-friendly websites. Eager to continuously improve and build innovative solutions.

Personal Mission

I strived to create a more positive environment for those in need. Recognizing the importance of basic necessities, I donated food and clothing to those less fortunate. Additionally, I volunteered at a local foster care center, contributing to a sense of well-being by cleaning and painting the premises. Through these efforts, I aimed to offer not just basic comfort, but also a brighter, more dignified space.

Life Goals

My life's journey weaves together my creative spirit and technical prowess. I dream of merging these passions, perhaps by contributing to the future of photography at a pioneering tech company like Sony or Canon. Beyond that, I yearn to explore the hidden corners of the world, venturing where few have dared to tread and collecting experiences that broaden my perspective.

PROJECTS

What projects have I done
